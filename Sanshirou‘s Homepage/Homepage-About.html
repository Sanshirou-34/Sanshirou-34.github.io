ABOUT ME

Hello! My name is Sanshirou . I am a Freelance Motion Designer based in Beijing,China.
I am now a sophomore student at Communication University of China, majoring in Advertising (Digital Marketing). From the moment I stepped into college, I gradually became interested in Motion Design and fell in love. So i taught myself 3D software from the end of 2020. I learned Cinema4d at first,and most recently Houdini. In the process of learning, I gradually came into contact with more auxiliary design software.
I enjoy coming up with creative ideas for advertising, experimenting with conceptual designs, and making amazing visuals serve creative storytelling. I'm proud of helping others identify and solve problems, enjoy team brainstorming, I appreciate being inspired by those around me.
For me, there is still a long way to go, and I will continue to learn...
